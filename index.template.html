How It Works

Journeys straight from the blueprint

Worker Flow

Find verified work fast

  1. Create your account, verify identity, and build a trusted work profile.
  2. Set skills, availability, and location sharing to unlock better nearby matches.
  3. Apply, check in with QR, complete jobs, and withdraw earnings from wallet.
Employer Flow

Hire crews with less risk

  1. Register your company and upload business verification documents.
  2. Post jobs with skills, pay, location, and headcount, then review AI-ranked candidates.
  3. Fund escrow, manage live jobs, confirm completion, and rate workers in-app.
